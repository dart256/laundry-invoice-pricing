FAQ

Common questions

Does it work without internet?

Yes — the app is built offline-first. NEPA cuts won't affect your work. Data saves locally on your device and syncs when connection returns (Ultra plan).

What devices does it work on?

Any device with a browser — Android phones, iPhones, tablets, laptops and desktop PCs. It installs like a real app directly from the browser.

How does setup work?

After payment, we configure the app for your business — your name, address, phone, and full pricing catalog. You get a private link to your own instance, ready to use.

Can I connect a thermal printer?

Yes. Any 80mm thermal printer connected to your device works via the browser print dialog. No special drivers needed.

What if I need help after setup?

Premium clients get standard support via WhatsApp. Ultra clients get priority response — we treat your issues as urgent.

Can I upgrade from Premium to Ultra later?

Yes — you pay the difference in setup fee and we migrate your data to the cloud. No data is lost.
